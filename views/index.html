QueueItUp

A beautiful music queueing app that is as easy to use as it is on the eyes

QueueItUp is an android application that can be used at your next party for queueing music on the host's device. (Spotify Premium needed)

Why Choose QueueItUp?

QueueItUp is the easiest way to show everyone your inner DJ.

Create Party

To create a party, simply give it a name and a password which your users will use to join.

Get your friends to join!

Tell your friends the password and get the party rolling.

Connects directly to Spotify.

Playing the music off your laptop? No problem. QueueItUp works with Spotify Connect which allows you to queue music for any device.

Beautiful Design

Simple, straight-to-the-point design makes it easier than ever to make your next party the most democratic ever.
